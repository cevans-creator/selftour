## Typical Results
- 10-community builder: saves $8,000-15,000/month, captures 10-20 additional tours/month

## Notes for Sales Team
- Always let the builder fill in their own numbers — don't guess their costs
- Focus on revenue capture, not just cost savings — builders care about sales more than ops savings
- The "after-hours tours" stat (40%) is the killer data point — it's immediately relatable
- If they push back on the 8-12% conversion rate, use 5% — the math still works
