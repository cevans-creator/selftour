# KeySherpa — Positioning Document

## One-Liner
KeySherpa is self-guided tour software for homebuilders — smart locks, AI, and automation so buyers tour 24/7 while your sales team focuses on closing.

## The Problem
Homebuilders lose qualified prospects because they can't offer tours outside business hours. Model home staffing is expensive ($50-80K/agent/year), and most builders can't justify a full-time presence at every community. Prospects who can't see a home on their schedule go to a competitor who lets them.

## The Solution
KeySherpa automates the entire self-guided tour experience:
- **Smart lock hubs** ship pre-configured — plug in, pair your lock from the dashboard, done
- **Automated access codes** generate 15 min before each tour and delete at the end
- **AI visitor assistant** answers prospect questions via SMS during the tour
- **Full communication sequence** — confirmation, reminders, access code, follow-up, nurture
- **Analytics** — who toured, when, which properties, conversion rates, lead sources

## Why KeySherpa vs. UTour
| | KeySherpa | UTour |
|---|---|---|
| Hardware | Our hub ships ready. Plug in, pair from dashboard. | Requires SmartThings hub + separate lock setup |
| Lock support | Z-Wave direct (Kwikset, Schlage, Yale) — no middleware | Seam/SmartThings middleware (extra point of failure) |
| AI assistant | Claude-powered SMS Q&A during tours | Basic chatbot (AtlasRTX add-on, extra cost) |
| Onboarding | Under 1 hour, self-service | Weeks, requires their team involvement |
| Pricing | Transparent per-community pricing | Opaque, requires sales call, $15K+/mo for 70 properties |
| CRM integration | Webhook to any CRM (Zapier, HubSpot, Salesforce) | Limited |
| ID verification | Photo ID (Stripe Identity) + credit card option | Credit/debit card only |

## Target Customer
- **Primary:** Regional homebuilders with 5-200 active communities (production builders)
- **Secondary:** Custom home builders and real estate brokerages with model homes
- **Sweet spot:** 10-50 communities, currently staffing model homes or using UTour and unhappy with cost/complexity
- **Decision maker:** VP of Sales, VP of Marketing, Director of New Home Sales
- **Champion:** Community Sales Manager (the person who feels the pain daily)

## Key Value Props (in order of importance to builders)
1. **Revenue capture** — tours happen at 8pm, Sunday mornings, whenever the buyer wants. You're open 24/7.
2. **Cost reduction** — reduce model home staffing costs by 40-60%. One agent covers multiple communities.
3. **Speed** — live in under an hour. No IT department needed. No contractors.
4. **Data** — know exactly who toured, when, how long, and which homes interested them. Feed your CRM automatically.
5. **Security** — ID verification, time-limited codes, full audit trail. More secure than a lockbox.

## Objection Handling
**"We already use UTour"**
→ "How much are you paying per community? Most builders we talk to are paying $200+/community/month with UTour. Our hardware is a one-time cost and the software is significantly less. Plus you get AI Q&A and direct Z-Wave lock control — no SmartThings middleware."

**"Our buyers want a person there"**
→ "Your best buyers want to see the home on their schedule. 40% of self-guided tours happen outside business hours. KeySherpa doesn't replace your sales team — it captures the prospects your team would have missed."

**"What if someone damages the property?"**
→ "Every visitor is ID-verified before they get an access code. You have their name, photo ID, phone, and email. Time-locked codes mean they can only enter during their window. It's actually more secure than an unlocked model with a sign-in sheet."

**"We don't want to deal with technology"**
→ "You plug in a box and press one button on your lock. That's the entire hardware setup. Everything else is managed from a browser — your team can learn it in 10 minutes."
