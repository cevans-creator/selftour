# Texas Private Homebuilders — Prospect List

**Compiled:** 2026-04-17
**Scope:** Privately held homebuilders with 5+ active communities in Texas. Excludes publicly traded builders and subsidiaries of public companies. Sourced exclusively from public web research — LinkedIn URLs, community counts, and vendor info are cited where verifiable and flagged where not.

**How to read this list:**
- **Section A — Confirmed prospects.** Independently private, TX HQ or substantial TX HQ presence, 5+ communities verified. These are priority outreach targets.
- **Section B — Candidates.** TX presence with 5+ communities, but need human verification before outreach (private-parent ownership, HQ outside TX, or unclear current status).
- **Section C — Explicitly excluded.** Names you may have in mind that do NOT qualify as privately held (public parent or recent acquisition).

**Data caveats (read before using):**
- Community counts fluctuate monthly. Numbers shown are from aggregator snapshots (NewHomeSource, Homes.com) and builder websites at the time of research — treat as "order of magnitude," not month-end accurate.
- Self-tour vendor column: most builders do not publicly disclose their vendor. "Unknown" means no public signal found — it does not mean they don't use one. Positive signals ("Confirmed: UTour") come from builder websites or press releases.
- LinkedIn URLs were only recorded when I could match a specific named individual to a role in public sources. Many are listed as "Name only — LinkedIn not confirmed" rather than risk a wrong URL.
